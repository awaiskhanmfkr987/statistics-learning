Parametric Tests

Assumptions: Normal distribution, equal variances, interval/ratio data. Examples: t-tests, ANOVA.

Non-Parametric Tests

Used when assumptions of parametric tests are not met. Examples: Mann-Whitney U, Wilcoxon, Kruskal-Wallis.

Categorical / Distribution Tests

Examples: Chi-Square, Binomial Test, Sign Test, Kolmogorov-Smirnov.
